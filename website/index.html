Everything your Mac needs

8 cleanup categories, one reversible workflow. All on-device.

🧹

System Junk

Caches, logs, and language files that macOS regenerates on demand. Protected system paths excluded automatically.

📦

Uninstaller

Remove apps with all their preferences, caches, and support files. Surfaces leftovers from deleted apps.

👯

Duplicates

Find duplicate files across Downloads, Documents, and Desktop. Pare keeps the best copy automatically.

🗂️

Large & Old Files

Files over 100 MB unopened in 6+ months. You decide what to keep — nothing is pre-selected.

🛠️

Developer Junk

Xcode DerivedData, unused simulators, node_modules, and build caches. The biggest single reclaim category.

✉️

Mail Cleanup

Apple Mail keeps attachments on disk forever. Safe to remove — Mail re-downloads on demand.

📸

Photos

Screenshots, RAW originals, burst near-duplicates, and Photos library cache.

🗺️

Space Lens

A visual treemap of what's taking up your disk. Click any block to drill into that category.

🔄

Recovery Bin

Everything goes to a 30-day Recovery Bin first. Restore with one click. Nothing is permanent.
